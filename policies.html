Welcome to McHub. This page contains our Privacy Policy and User Policy.

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 10 Sep 2025

McHub (“we,” “our,” or “us”) provides community, remittance comparison, service booking, and communication features for Myanmar people in Thailand and abroad. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application and related services.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Phone number (OTP) and user ID from Firebase.
  • Profile Data: Name, photo, bio (optional).
  • Booking Data: Service type, notes, contact info.
  • Remittance Data: Country/currency selections; no payment data stored.
  • Location Data: Approximate or precise location for nearby services.
  • Chat Messages: Stored securely; anonymized on account deletion.
  • Device Info: Crash logs, identifiers, analytics.
  • Localization: Language preference (English/Myanmar).

2. How We Use Information

  • Provide login, profile, bookings, and remittance features.
  • Show nearby services and places.
  • Enable messaging between users.
  • Improve app performance and security.
  • Send relevant notifications.

3. Third-Party Services

Includes Firebase (Auth, Firestore, Storage, Crashlytics, Analytics). Facebook SDK is bundled but disabled in production.

4. Data Storage & Security

Data encrypted in transit, stored securely. No sale of personal data.

5. Retention & Deletion

Users can delete accounts anytime; personal data is removed, messages anonymized.

6. Rights

Access, update, or delete your data. Withdraw consent by deleting account.

7. Children

No services for children under 13.
